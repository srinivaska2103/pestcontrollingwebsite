Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and services.

Are there any hidden charges?

No, our pricing is 100% transparent. The price we quote after your inspection or through our custom plans is the final price you pay. There are no surprise fees or hidden costs.

Is the treatment safe for children and pets?

Yes! We prioritize your family's safety by using eco-friendly, industry-approved products. Once the treatment has dried (usually within 1-2 hours), it is completely safe for children and pets to return to the area.

How long does the service take?

Most standard residential treatments take between 1 to 2 hours. However, the exact duration depends heavily on the size of your property and the specific type of pest being treated.

Do you provide a warranty?

Yes, all of our comprehensive treatment plans come with a standard satisfaction guarantee. If pests return between your scheduled visits, we will come back and re-treat the area at absolutely no extra cost.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Absolutely. We understand that plans change. Just let us know at least 24 hours in advance, and we will happily reschedule your appointment to a more convenient time without any cancellation fees.

Which payment methods are accepted?

We accept a wide variety of convenient payment methods including major Credit/Debit Cards, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Net Banking, and cash at the time of service delivery.

Is commercial pest control available?

Yes, we provide highly customized, FDA/OSHA-compliant commercial pest control plans tailored specifically for offices, restaurants, hotels, warehouses, and factories.

Do I need to leave my home during treatment?

In most standard service cases, you do not need to vacate the premises. However, for specialized, intensive treatments like heavy bed bug or flea infestations, we may advise you to leave the home for 2-4 hours.
