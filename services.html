Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pest control services.

How long does the treatment take?
Most standard residential treatments take between 1 to 2 hours, depending on the size of your property and the extent of the pest issue. Commercial treatments or extensive termite control may take longer.
Is it safe for children and pets?
Yes, absolutely. We use eco-friendly and industry-approved products that are tough on pests but completely safe for your family, including children and pets. We recommend keeping pets away from treated areas until they are dry (usually 1-2 hours).
Do I need to leave during treatment?
For general pest control, you usually do not need to leave your home. However, for specific treatments like extensive fogging or heavy chemical applications, we may ask you to vacate the premises for a few hours. We will always inform you beforehand.
How often should I schedule pest control?
For continuous protection, we recommend quarterly (every 3 months) pest control services. However, if you are experiencing a severe infestation, more frequent initial visits may be required until the problem is under control.
Do you provide a warranty?
Yes! We stand behind our work. Most of our treatments come with a 30 to 90-day warranty. If pests return within the warranty period, we will re-treat the affected areas at no additional cost.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash. Payment is typically collected after the service is completed to your satisfaction.
How soon can you visit?
We offer rapid response times. In most cases, we can schedule an inspection or treatment within 24-48 hours. For emergencies, same-day service may be available depending on your location.
